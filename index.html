Privacy Policy

This RTBT experience collects and processes personal data to guide you through a five‑question journey and create your personalised Heart Roadmap. We collect your email address, name, language preference and the answers you provide to our questions. These details are used solely to generate and deliver your Heart Roadmap, improve the quality of the experience and to contact you with your results.

We process speech and text using third‑party services (including OpenAI for language understanding and Whisper for speech recognition, and ElevenLabs for text‑to‑speech) via Make.com. Your audio and text data may be transferred to these providers for processing. We store your information in our data storage and Google Sheets for up to 30 days after your session. After this period your personal data will either be deleted or anonymised.

We will never sell your personal data or share it with third parties other than the service providers described above. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at privacy@rtbt.example. By starting this experience you consent to the collection and use of your data as outlined in this policy (consent version 1.0).
